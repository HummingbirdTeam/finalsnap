What to include

Small report, faster fix.

  • FinalSnap version if you can see it in Chrome extension details.
  • Browser version, operating system, and whether browser zoom was changed.
  • The exact workflow step: capture, Cut Middle, Smart Erase, export, Account, or install.
  • A screenshot or screen recording only if it is safe for you to share.
  • Your account email only for Pro, trial, reward, billing, or receipt questions.
