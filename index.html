Local-first by default.

FinalSnap is designed so screenshot image editing happens locally in your browser. Screenshot image data is not uploaded to FinalSnap servers by the extension.

  • Screenshot history and editor data stay in your Chrome profile unless you delete them.
  • Optional Pro, tester, support, payment, or promotion workflows use only the minimal data needed for that workflow.
  • FinalSnap does not load remote JavaScript or remote WebAssembly into the extension.
