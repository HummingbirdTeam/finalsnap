Privacy Policy

Local-first screenshot cleanup.

FinalSnap is built for evidence work. Screenshot pixels are processed locally by default. Optional account, support, payment, promotion, and analytics workflows use only the data needed for that workflow.

Last updated: May 24, 2026.

No screenshot upload by default.

Capture, Cut Middle, Smart Erase, crop, markup, and export run in the browser unless you choose an optional workflow.

Account data has a job.

Account, license, trial, payment, reward, and support data is used to operate those features.

Analytics avoids page content.

When enabled, analytics is for reliability and adoption signals, not screenshot pixels or browsing history.

Core Privacy Promise

FinalSnap is a Chrome extension for cleaning long webpage screenshots. The extension's main editing work happens locally in your browser. FinalSnap does not upload screenshot image data or webpage content by default.

Data Processed Locally

FinalSnap may process and store the following data locally in your browser:

  • Screenshot images captured from pages you choose to capture.
  • Edited image data, crop/cut/erase state, export preferences, and screenshot history.
  • Source URL, capture date, and user-supplied source context when you choose to keep it.
  • Local settings, trial state, feature-use counters, and license cache used to decide whether Pro features are available.

Optional Account, License, Payment, And Reward Data

If Pro licensing, trial access, paid features, tester access, beta access, redemption codes, or reward workflows are enabled, FinalSnap may contact a server to verify access and operate the selected workflow.

  • Installation ID or account ID used for license and abuse-prevention checks.
  • Email address or login provider identity when you create or sign in to an account.
  • Plan, license, trial, reward, and remaining Pro-day status.
  • Payment-provider identifiers, checkout status, subscription status, receipt metadata, and billing-management links when a real payment exists.
  • Promotion claim details submitted by you, such as platform, public post URL, requested reward days, account email, and claim notes.

Optional Product Analytics

FinalSnap may collect limited product analytics to understand version adoption, feature reliability, export success, account funnel health, and bug patterns. Analytics, when enabled, does not upload screenshot pixels, webpage content, captured page URLs, continuous browsing history, raw clickstream, mouse movement, or keystrokes.

Support And Feedback

If you send a support email, bug report, beta request, tester request, or feedback message, FinalSnap may receive the contact information and diagnostic details you choose to send. You should share screenshot content only when you decide it is needed for support.

Payments

Payments are handled by third-party payment providers. FinalSnap should receive only the payment status and metadata needed to activate, refresh, manage, or troubleshoot Pro access. Full card numbers or bank details are handled by the payment provider, not by the extension.

Chrome Permissions

  • activeTab, scripting, and related capture permissions are used when you choose to capture a page.
  • storage and unlimitedStorage are used for local settings, screenshot history, edited image data, export preferences, trial state, and license state.
  • downloads is used when you export or save a file.
  • Host permissions are used only for pages you choose to capture or interact with through FinalSnap.

Third-Party Services

FinalSnap may use third-party services only for workflows that need them:

  • Chrome Web Store for extension distribution and updates.
  • Payment providers for checkout, billing, subscription status, and receipts.
  • Login or email providers for account sign-in, verification, support, or product messages.
  • Hosting and database providers for account, license, reward, support, and analytics services.

Remote Code

The extension's core runtime code is packaged with the Chrome extension. Optional server pages such as Account, checkout, or support run as web pages outside the packaged extension.

Data Sharing

FinalSnap does not sell personal data. Data is shared only when needed to operate the extension's purpose, manage Pro access, process payments, deliver support, prevent abuse, comply with law, or complete an action requested by the user.

User Controls And Deletion

  • Delete local FinalSnap data through Chrome extension storage or browser controls.
  • Avoid optional account, support, payment, promotion, or analytics workflows when you do not want to send related data.
  • Request deletion of account, support, promotion, reward, or license records by emailing finalsnapapp@gmail.com.

Data Retention

Local screenshot and editing data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the extension. Server-side account, license, payment-status, support, promotion, reward, and analytics records are retained only as long as needed for product operation, support, abuse prevention, legal compliance, and business records.

Children's Privacy

FinalSnap is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated as FinalSnap evolves. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact finalsnapapp@gmail.com.

Privacy is part of the product promise: clean evidence without unnecessary upload by default.
